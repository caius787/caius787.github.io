Audience seating reminder

Students sit with their parents or family between performances.

Do not sit as one large student group. Be an attentive audience for every ensemble and support each other's concert work.

What to wear

  • Chinook music polo
  • Black pants or long black skirt
  • Black socks
  • Black shoes

Schedule

1:20-2:30 PM

Volunteers needed: Set up the main gym as soon as possible.

6:15-6:25 PM

Students arrive at Chinook, get instruments from the band room, and go to the main gym.

6:25 PM

All musicians in the main gym. Do not play yet.

6:30-8:00 PM

Concert begins earlier than usual. Please be ready and seated.

About 8:00 PM

Return chairs, stands, and percussion to the band room. Each student carries one item back before leaving.

Before performing

Leave your case and belongings in your locker and bring your music with you. Groups warm up together; no one warms up or plays alone. After performing, return to sit with your parents or family and pay attention to the other groups.

Setup reference

Stage and seating

Concert rows are arranged front to back as 15, 16, 18, 18, 17. Every chair gets a stand, and all percussion needs a stand.
