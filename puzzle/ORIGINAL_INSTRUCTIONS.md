 Plates: There are 4 Plates
Each plate has a 4x4 dimple surface
the 4x4 dimple surface acts as a holding  surface for the round Piece shapes
Each plate is 2 sided with once side black and  other side white
Each side has 1 or 2 obstructions that prevent the a Piece shapes from fitting into that slot
Black side have white obstructions, white sides have black obstructions Each Plate square is rotatable
Each plate square is designed to connect with 1 or more squares.

Dimple placement:
Each  slot dimple of the 4x4 grid on the face of the plate will be assigned a slot number.

|13 14 15 16| The first row is:  1  2  3   4 2nd:  5  6  7  8 3rd: 9 10 11 12 4th: 13 14 15 16   Plate layouts: Location of the slot obstructions: White: Plate 1: 1, 4 Plate 2: 14, 15 Plate 3: 2 Plate 4: 2,16  Black Plate: Plate 1: 8, 16
Plate 2: 4, 11

Plate 4: 7,16  Connected Plate orientations:
Plates can be rotated and connected on any side, connecting all four plates.
Each plate can be connected to one or 2 or 3 other plates, creating 7 shapes that look like a "L",  "reverse L", "T", "I", "z", "reverse z" or square, edges align.   In this case plate 1 row |1,2,3,4| will align horizontally with plate 2 row |1,2,3,4|  Additionally,  each plate can be offset by 2 dimple rows, to create 4 additional plate layouts.
"Pinwheel" -  2 by 2 hole in the center. 4x 4 grid offset by 2 dimple rows.
"Cross" - 2 plates connected on one side. A plate on top and bottom that sit centered between the first 2 "Z shape" - 2 sets of 2  plates connected on one side. The 2 sets are connected to each but offset by 2 dimple rows
"Revere Z shape" - 2 sets of 2  plates connected on one side. The 2 sets are connected to each but offset by 2 dimple rows in the other direct than the Z shape.

Piece Shapes
All the Piece shapes are balls that fit into the dimples on Connected Plates.  All Piece shapes can be rotated.
All Piece shapes can be flipped
All Piece shapes cross plate borders to fill one or more dimples.
All Piece shapes must avoid obstructions There are a total of 12 Piece shapes All Piece shapes have an assigned color All Piece shapes are pre-defined, and unchangeable

  Game objective:   Combine the four plates into the 11 Connected Plate orientations or game board shapes. Fill in all the open dimples with the All Piece shapes and no game piece can sit on an obstruction.  The player chooses one game Piece at a time and can place it.
The Piece can be flipped or rotated.
A Pice can not be place on a grid shape.
